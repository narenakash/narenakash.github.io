Research

Ongoing projects and areas of interest. For published work, see Publications.

Ongoing at AIG Hospitals

Multilingual Ambient Consultation Scribing, Clinical Coding and Order Management

Principal investigator: Naren Akash

A large-scale real-world deployment of over 1,000 consultations daily across 20 departments in 10+ Indian languages, used by 100+ superspecialists. The system enables multilingual ambient scribing, real-time ICD coding and structured order generation, with traceability from summary to source audio, medical text normalization, and accent/dialect robustness, powered by a context-adaptive reasoning engine.

Digitization of Paper-Based ECGs for Cardiovascular Risk Prediction

Principal investigators: Naren Akash and Sayan Kahali

Designed for Global South settings where paper-based ECGs are prevalent. Combines smartphone-based capture and digitization with downstream AI for early detection of cardiovascular disease, enabling cost-effective mass screening via the Asian Healthcare Foundation.

Foundation Model for Abdominal Ultrasound Acquisition and Interpretation

Principal investigator: Naren Akash

Building a generalizable model for understanding and guiding real-time abdominal ultrasound, enabling acquisition support, anatomy-aware parsing and report structuring for radiology and hepatology.

Vision-Language Models for Multimodal Oncology Imaging

Principal investigator: Naren Akash

Developing unified models to jointly reason over PET-CT, MRI and reports for cancer staging and treatment response monitoring. Explores structure-function fusion in GI and hepatobiliary cancers.

AI-Augmented Clinical Workflow Platform for Radiology Reporting

Principal investigator: Naren Akash

Designing an integrated platform combining dictation, templating, guideline-based field generation and lesion-aware multimedia reporting. Incorporates patient-centered outputs and radiologist-in-the-loop optimization.

Ongoing at IIIT Hyderabad

Segmental-Anatomy Inspired Graph Models for 3D Lung CT Pathology Classification

With Rudransh Agarwal and Jayanthi Sivaswamy

Developing an anatomy-inspired graph model of the lung based on morphology from 3D CT volumes to analyze spatial distribution and texture patterns of diffuse parenchymal diseases using graph neural networks.

End-to-End Free-Text Guided Pathology Segmentation in Medical Images

With Shreyas Reddy Palley and Jayanthi Sivaswamy

Formulating methods to effectively integrate free-text medical reports — more the norm in a clinical setting than structured reports — into segmentation pipelines for improved pathology delineation.

Exploiting Spatial Relationships as an Explicit Prior for 3D Anatomy Segmentation

With Soham Vatsal Vaishnav and Jayanthi Sivaswamy

Leveraging the inherent spatial arrangement of organs in the human body as an explicit prior to enhance segmentation performance in challenging medical images.

Anatomy Aware, Controllable 3D Brain Volume Generation

With Anirudh Kaushik and Jayanthi Sivaswamy

Developing methods for synthesizing 3D brain scans with improved controllability to manipulate anatomical and imaging features.

Select Completed Projects

Multimodal “Expert-in-the-Loop” Healthcare Chatbot Using MLLMs and MM-RAG

With Mohit Jain (Microsoft Research)

Designed a multimodal WhatsApp-based chatbot leveraging multimodal large language models and retrieval-augmented generation to meet the information needs of community health workers in India.

vEHR: Voice-Based Patient and Doctor Facing Health Records for the Margins

With Ananditha Raghunath and Mohit Jain (Microsoft Research)

Developed voice-based technologies integrated with LLMs in a WhatsApp-based chatbot to optimize clinical documentation by enabling the creation, editing and verification of health records from consultation audio.
