Hey, I'm Naren Akash

I'm an AI Research Scientist at AIG Hospitals. I lead the AI and biomedical informatics research in the Department of Cardiology and Electrophysiology at the Cardiac Sciences Center for Excellence, working with clinician-researchers and data scientists with an aim to optimize the practice of clinical medicine, care delivery and health system operations.

Previously, I was a research intern at Microsoft Research with Mohit Jain. I explored the use of multimodal LLMs and retrieval augmentation for "expert-in-the-loop" healthcare chatbots. I also developed algorithms for automated cataract surgery video analysis in collaboration with Sankara Eye Foundation for surgical training.

I'm pursuing both my Bachelor of Technology and MS by Research in Computer Science (currently, on-leave) from IIIT Hyderabad. I was fortunate to have worked with and mentored by Jayanthi Sivaswamy at the Medical Image Analysis Lab in Center for Visual Information Technology since 2020. I had spent wonderful years taking the baby steps of research wandering through the diverse areas of medical information processing in an inter-disciplinary team: from developing AI-driven imaging-based diagnostic tools to creating visualization software for medical education.

Driven by the promise of clinical machine intelligence, I'm fascinated by the potential of approaching medical challenges through a computational lens. I work at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and medical imaging. My interest extends to the latest advancements in deep learning and its diverse applications that can create a tangible, positive impact on society.

Beyond research, I actively participate in the broader medical imaging community. For over four years, I've been involved with the MICCAI Student Board, currently serving as its President. Previously, I interned at INAI - Intel Labs, working on developing deep learning-assisted early oral cancer detection algorithms from smartphone camera images. Earlier, I was an early team member at Matchday.AI, building automated football field top-view registration and analytics tools for sports broadcast videos.
